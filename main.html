Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean. The destination is known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and amazing marine life.

  1. Snorkeling and Diving: Explore the underwater world of Maldives, and spot a wide variety of fish and marine life.
  2. Island Hopping: Visit different islands and explore the local culture, and enjoy water sports activities.
  3. Sunset Cruise: Watch the sunset on a traditional boat, and enjoy a romantic dinner under the stars.
  4. Relaxing on the Beach: Lay back and soak up the sun on the beautiful beaches of Maldives.
  5. Spa Treatment: Indulge in a relaxing spa treatment, and rejuvenate your body and mind.
  • Seafood: Maldives is famous for its fresh seafood dishes, including fish curry, grilled fish, and lobster.
  • Hedhikaa: A traditional Maldivian snack made of fish, coconut, and spices.
  • Boshi Mashuni: A popular breakfast dish made of grated coconut, tuna, and chilli.
  • Mas Huni: Another breakfast dish made of shredded smoked fish and coconut.
  • Rihaakuru: A traditional Maldivian fish paste used as a condiment for many dishes.
